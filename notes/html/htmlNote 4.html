Hyperlinks

Hyperlink is something you can click and takes you to another page.

  • <a> (Anchor)

    • Marks the beginning and the end of a hypertext link

    • href (Hyperlink Reference)

      • Specifies the destination.

      • Method 1
        Link to a website
