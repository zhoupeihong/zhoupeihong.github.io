Recipe

Eggs and Tomatos

List of Ingredients:

  • Water
  • two eggs
  • one tomato
  • oil
  • salt

This is a receipe for some delicious food

Steps to follow:

  1. peel off the skin of the tomato

  2. chop the tomato into appropriate pieces

  3. break the shell of the eggs and pour the yoke to a bowl

  4. put salt in the bowl and mix the yoke and salt

  5. put the oil into a pan and heat it up

  6. put the eggs into the pan

  7. put the tomato into the pan

  8. enjoy your food!
